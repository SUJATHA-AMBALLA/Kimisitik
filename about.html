From EIT to Global Impact

Kimitisik was founded by learners who experienced firsthand the power of applied innovation. They saw a critical need to connect academic research with real-world entrepreneurial action. Today, our team of academics, engineers, and expert facilitators operates at the intersection of education and industry, empowering people to create tangible impact.

Fun fact: “ki mit iszik” (‘who drinks what?’ in Hungarian) — our playful name origin — remains a small cultural note that reminds us of our collaborative roots.
