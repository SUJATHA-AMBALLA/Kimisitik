Our Proven Challenge-Based Learning Process

Our CBL model, aligned with award-winning Dutch universities, guides your students through a proven process: from ambiguity to impact.

  1. Engage: We start with a real-world, “wicked problem” from an industry partner.
  2. Investigate: Teams collaborate to define the problem and research potential solutions.
  3. Act: They build, test, and iterate a real prototype.
  4. Present: Teams pitch their solution to a panel of real-world experts, partners, and investors.
